Info

I made this because I prefer reading PDFs. Reading long stuff on websites is annoying, and it is way easier to keep track of where you stopped in a pdf reader.

The goal was just to put these routes in one place in a format that is easier to download and read. Some pdfs were made by me, and some were found elsewhere.

If anything here should not be up, contact me through the email on the DMCA page and I will remove it.
